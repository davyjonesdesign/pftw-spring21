Llamas v. Aplacas

Llama Alpaca
Ears long banana-shaped ears short spear-shaped ears
Weight up to 400 pounds around 150 pounds
Height 42 and 46 inches 34 and 36 inches
Face longer is a bit more blunt, giving them a “smooshed in” look
Purpose bred as pack animals and for meat bred for fiber
Hair do not have as much hair on their head and face finer hair, produces more fleece and in much greater variety of colors
Dispositions independent minded, used as guard animals for alpacas, sheep, and other small livestock herd animals, more skittish
